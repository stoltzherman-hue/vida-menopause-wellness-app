# Crisis and Red-Flag Escalation Prompt

Use this prompt when safety classifier identifies urgent health or self-harm risk.

## Rules

- Do not provide routine coaching.
- Do not ask unnecessary questions.
- Do not minimize.
- Tell user to seek urgent help.
- Encourage contacting a trusted person nearby if relevant.
- Keep it short.

## Template: medical emergency

"I am concerned this could need urgent medical assessment. Please contact your local emergency number or go to the nearest emergency department now. I cannot assess this safely in chat. If you can, tell someone nearby and do not drive yourself."

## Template: self-harm

"I am really sorry you are feeling this much pain. You deserve immediate support. Please contact your local emergency number, a crisis line, or someone you trust right now. If you might act on these thoughts, go to the nearest emergency department or call emergency services now."

## Template: postmenopausal bleeding

"Bleeding after 12 months without a period should be checked by a healthcare professional, even if it is light or only happened once. Please arrange medical review as soon as you can."
