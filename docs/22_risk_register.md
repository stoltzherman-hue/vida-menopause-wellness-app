# Risk Register

## Product risks

### Users do not trust AI with health data

Mitigation:

- Clear privacy controls.
- Consent-based AI.
- Explain what data is used.
- Allow memory deletion.

### Tracking feels like work

Mitigation:

- Under two-minute check-in.
- One-tap logging.
- Personalize symptom list.
- Weekly insight reward.

### Community quality declines

Mitigation:

- Strong guidelines.
- Reporting.
- Moderation tooling.
- Seed positive culture.
- Circle size limits.

## Medical safety risks

### AI gives medical advice

Mitigation:

- Strict prompts.
- Safety classifier.
- Red-flag tests.
- Refusal templates.
- Human review process.

### Users delay care for red flags

Mitigation:

- Prominent escalation.
- Red-flag education.
- Doctor-prep not diagnosis.

## Privacy risks

### Health data exposed in community

Mitigation:

- Separate profiles.
- Warning before posting sensitive data.
- No automatic sharing.

### PHI in logs

Mitigation:

- Logging policy.
- Redaction middleware.
- Developer training.

## Commercial risks

### Premium value unclear

Mitigation:

- Make AI companion and report export core premium moments.
- Show insights preview.
- Annual discount.

### High AI costs

Mitigation:

- Usage caps.
- Summarized context.
- Model routing.
- Cache static content.

## Compliance risks

### HIPAA/GDPR/POPIA obligations underestimated

Mitigation:

- Legal review before launch in regulated markets.
- Privacy-by-design controls.
- Vendor review.
- Data processing records.

## Technical risks

### JSONB overuse causes analytics pain

Mitigation:

- Normalize key events.
- Use JSONB only for flexible metadata.

### Voice creates privacy concerns

Mitigation:

- Push-to-talk.
- Clear recording indicator.
- No always listening.
- Transcript control.
