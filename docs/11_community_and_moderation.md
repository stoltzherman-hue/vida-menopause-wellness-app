# Community and Moderation

## Community promise

Vida's community is a free, supportive place where women can share lived experience without shame. It is not a replacement for medical care.

## Community features

- Topic forums.
- Anonymous/pseudonymous profiles.
- Replies.
- Reactions.
- Bookmarks.
- Reporting.
- Private Circles.
- Expert Q&A later.

## Default categories

- Start Here.
- Hot Flash Hacks.

- Brain Fog and Work.
- Mood and Anxiety.
- HRT Experiences.

- Surgical Menopause.
- Perimenopause Period Changes.
- Sex, Intimacy, and Vaginal Health.
- Weight, Strength, and Energy.
- Dating and Relationships.
- Career and Menopause.
- Wins and Encouragement.

## Circle design

Circle size: 8-12.

Matching factors:

- Menopause stage.
- Primary symptoms.
- Goals.
- Timezone preference.
- Privacy preference.
- Life context optional: career, parenting, caregiving, single/dating, surgical menopause.

Circle rules:

- Private posts visible to members only.
- Health tracking data is not shared automatically.
- Members can leave anytime.
- Report function available inside Circle.

## Moderation principles

- Protect vulnerable users.
- Allow lived experience.
- Do not allow dangerous medical instructions.
- Do not allow harassment, shame, sexual exploitation, scams, or hate.
- Do not allow miracle cures or misinformation.
- Use warnings, content labels, removal, and bans based on severity.

## Content labels

- Lived experience.
- Needs evidence check.
- Sensitive topic.
- Possible urgent care issue.
- Medication/HRT discussion.

## Prohibited content

- Direct instructions to start/stop/change medication.
- Claims that a cure is guaranteed.
- Selling unverified supplements or treatments.
- Harassment or personal attacks.
- Sexual solicitation.
- Doxxing or sharing personal information.
- Self-harm encouragement.
- Hate or discrimination.

## Moderation workflow

1. User reports content.
2. Automated safety triage checks category.
3. High-risk content hidden pending review if needed.
4. Moderator reviews.
5. Action: no action, label, edit request, remove, warn, suspend, ban.
6. Audit log created.
7. User appeal allowed for removals/suspensions.

## Misinformation handling

Do not shame the poster. Use:

- Soft label.
- Link to evidence-based educational content.
- Invite users to speak to qualified clinician.
- Remove if dangerous or repeated.

## Community AI support

AI can:

- Suggest relevant categories.
- Draft a supportive reply.
- Warn user before posting private health data.
- Flag possible misinformation for moderation.

AI must not:

- Auto-post on user's behalf without explicit confirmation.
- Present community stories as evidence.
- Expose private tracker data.
