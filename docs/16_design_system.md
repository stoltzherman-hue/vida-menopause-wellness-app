# Design System

## Brand feel

Vida should feel calm, wise, premium, and human. Avoid patronizing femininity. The design should support women at night, during work breaks, and before doctor appointments.

## Color palette

Use as starting tokens:

- Sage: `#8FAE9B`.
- Deep sage: `#4F6F5D`.
- Terracotta: `#C97B63`.
- Dusty rose: `#D8A6A6`.
- Soft cream: `#F7F1E8`.
- Warm sand: `#E8D8C3`.
- Charcoal: `#2F3430`.
- Soft gray: `#EEF0EC`.

## Typography

- Body minimum 16px.
- Comfortable line height 1.5-1.7.
- Avoid thin fonts.
- Use clear hierarchy.

## Components

Core:

- App shell.
- Bottom mobile nav.
- Check-in card.
- Symptom slider.
- Mood selector.
- Trigger chips.
- Insight card.
- Safety alert card.
- Trend chart card.
- Community post card.
- Circle card.
- AI chat bubble.
- Doctor report section.
- Medication schedule card.

## Accessibility

- WCAG 2.1 AA target.
- High contrast mode.
- Keyboard navigation.
- Screen reader labels.
- No color-only meaning.
- Large tap targets.
- Reduced motion support.

## Mobile navigation

Bottom nav:

- Today.
- Track.
- Vida AI.
- Community.
- More.

## Tone of copy

Use:

- "You are not alone."
- "Your experience is real."
- "Your data suggests..."
- "A helpful next step could be..."

Avoid:

- "Just menopause."
- "Normal for your age."
- Fear-based copy.
- Overly cute language.

## Dark mode

Dark mode is important for night sweats, insomnia, and bedtime companion use.

Requirements:

- No bright white backgrounds.
- Chart colors remain readable.
- AI chat input easy to use in low light.
- Sleep wind-down mode should be visually calm.
