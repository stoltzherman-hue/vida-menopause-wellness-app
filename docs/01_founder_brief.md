# Founder Brief - Vida

## Summary

Vida is a mobile-first menopause and perimenopause wellness platform designed to help women feel understood, supported, and prepared. It combines a free community with a premium AI companion and health intelligence system.

The core insight: menopause is not just a medical event. It affects sleep, work, relationships, confidence, sex, mood, body composition, energy, and identity. Most women need trustworthy education, pattern recognition, peer support, and help preparing for clinical conversations.

## Product positioning

Vida is not a telehealth clinic in the MVP. It is a self-management, education, community, and doctor-preparation platform.

Positioning statement:

> Vida helps women navigate perimenopause and menopause with private tracking, supportive community, and an AI companion that helps them understand patterns, prepare for doctors, and feel less alone.

## User segments

1. Early perimenopause, unsure what is happening.
2. Symptomatic perimenopause, juggling work/family and irregular cycles.
3. Menopause/post-menopause, managing sleep, hot flashes, mood, sexual health, and long-term health.
4. Surgical menopause or premature ovarian insufficiency, often needing more urgent support and specific education.
5. HRT-curious or HRT users, needing adherence, symptom correlation, and doctor-prep tools.

## Jobs to be done

- Help me understand whether my symptoms may fit the menopause transition.
- Help me track what is happening without making it a chore.
- Help me see patterns across symptoms, triggers, sleep, stress, medication, and lifestyle.
- Help me talk to my doctor with confidence.
- Help me talk to my partner, manager, or family without feeling dismissed.
- Help me meet women who understand.
- Help me feel supported at night when symptoms feel isolating.

## Differentiation

- Free community as mission and growth loop.
- AI companion designed for menopause-specific emotional and practical support.
- Pattern intelligence across symptoms, triggers, sleep, cycle, HRT, and lifestyle.
- Doctor-report generation.
- Strong privacy separation between public community identity and private health data.
- Tone that is mature, calm, direct, and empowering.

## Commercial model

- Free: community, basic tracker, limited AI, basic educational content.
- Premium: unlimited tracking, advanced AI insights, AI companion, voice, HRT/medication tracker, reports, enhanced reminders, advanced dashboards, personalized plans.
- Future: clinician-reviewed programs, employer benefit, partner content, telehealth referrals, expert events, and local provider directory.

## MVP success metrics

- Activation: 70% of new users complete onboarding or create community profile.
- Habit: 40% complete at least 4 check-ins in first 14 days.
- Community: 30% of activated users post, reply, or join a Circle in first 14 days.
- Premium conversion: 5%-10% from activated users after first 30 days.
- Retention: 30-day retained active users above 25% in early MVP.
- Safety: 100% of red-flag test cases route to escalation copy.
