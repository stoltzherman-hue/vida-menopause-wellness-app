## Article: Hot flashes and night sweats

Category: Vasomotor symptoms.
Tags: hot_flashes, night_sweats, sleep.

Summary:
Hot flashes and night sweats are common during the menopause transition. Tracking timing, severity, sleep disruption, alcohol, caffeine, stress, heat, and spicy food can help identify patterns worth discussing with a healthcare provider.

Disclaimer:
Educational only, not medical advice.

Category: Sleep.
Tags: sleep_disruption, insomnia, night_sweats.
